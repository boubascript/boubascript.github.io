General Membership
Guidelines

Being a member of the Macaulay Business Club will provide you with an exclusive network and unique opportunities. Although our events are open to the general CUNY population, all members will gain priority access and registration to all our events and opportunities.

To apply, you must be a current student at one of the following CUNY campuses:

  • Baruch
  • Brooklyn
  • City
  • Hunter
  • John Jay
  • Lehman
  • Queens
  • Staten Island

General Membership Requirements:
  • 1 published article per semester
  • 3 professional events per semester
  • 1 tour per semester
  • 2 co-sponsorships per semester
  • 4 Facebook flyer "shares" per semester
These are the requirements to be admitted into the Macaulay Business Club. Once admitted, general members must write 1 article and attend at least 2 professional events per semester. Your membership confirmation email will be sent after you attend 3 MBC or 3 MBC co-sponsored events.
