Planning Committee
Guidelines

Joining the Macaulay Business Club's planning committee will provide you with an exclusive network, an opportunity to gain valuable skills, and a chance to make an impact on a fast growing organization. We actively recruit the best and brightest students at CUNY. Our admissions process is rolling, but we encourage all applicants to apply as early into the semester as possible. Our application process is as follows:

  1. Fill out the application
  2. After an initial review, a representative from MBC will contact you for an interview
  3. You will receive an email regarding your membership with 5-7 days

Basic Qualifications:
  • Be a current student at one of the following CUNY Campuses:
    • Baruch
    • Brooklyn
    • City
    • Hunter
    • John Jay
    • Lehman
    • Queens
    • Staten Island
  • Maintain a 3.3 GPA (1st semester Freshman are exempt)
  • Ability to work in a team
  • Desire to make an impact

Planning Committee Requirements:
  • 2 published article per semester
  • 4 professional events per semester
  • Attend 90% of meetings
  • 2 tours per semester
  • 3 co-sponsorships per semester
  • 4 Facebook flyer "shares" per semester
